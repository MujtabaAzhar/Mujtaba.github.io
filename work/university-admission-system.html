University Admission Management System

Full admission pipeline for CUVAS — from application to merit list

THE PROBLEM

University admissions ran on paper forms, bank queues, and manual merit calculations. Applicants had no visibility into their status, and staff re-entered the same data at every stage.

THE STACK

  • Laravel 12
  • PHP 8.2
  • MySQL
  • PDF generation
  • Excel exports
  • OTP via email & WhatsApp

WHAT I BUILT

A complete admission platform for CUVAS with three roles. Students register with OTP verification (email and WhatsApp), fill applications with draft saving, upload documents, and print their form and bank fee challan. Admins manage admission cycles, departments, and programs, verify applications in bulk, record entry-test results with an automatic re-test loop (up to 3 attempts), and generate and publish merit lists. Department heads get read-only oversight, and every report exports to Excel.

THE OUTCOME

The entire admission cycle — application, verification, entry test, merit list, enrollment — runs through one system, with applicants tracking their own status instead of calling the office.
