Number Plate Recognition

Point a phone camera at a car — the plate number is read and ready to use

THE PROBLEM

Identifying a returning car meant typing its plate number by hand — slow at a busy branch, and one typo breaks the customer's history.

THE STACK

  • Mobile camera capture
  • OCR / image recognition
  • Laravel
  • REST API

WHAT I BUILT

A camera-based plate reader that runs on an ordinary mobile phone. Staff capture a photo of the car; the system extracts the plate's letters and numbers automatically and hands them to the rest of the platform — visit logging, loyalty lookup, and membership verification — with no manual typing.

THE OUTCOME

A car is identified in the time it takes to snap a photo. Plate entry errors disappear, and the front-line workflow got faster where it matters most: at the lane.
