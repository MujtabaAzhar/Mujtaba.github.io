02 — THE IOT STORY

One signal, followed all the way home

The Smart Tracker: custom hardware wired into car-wash machines in Saudi Arabia, counting every wash independently — even when the internet isn't.

  1. 01

    PLC

    A car gets washed

    The machine's PLC fires an electrical signal — invisible, unrecorded, gone in milliseconds.

  2. 02

    ESP32

    The tracker hears it

    A custom ESP32 board, wired straight into the PLC, catches the signal on the wire.

  3. 03

    BUFFER

    Offline? No problem

    No internet at the branch? Each wash is stored locally and queued.

  4. 04

    SERVER

    Synced to the cloud

    The moment the connection returns, everything auto-syncs to a central PHP/MySQL server. Zero washes lost.
